▶ TTS Reader

Ready. Copy any text anywhere, then tap 📋 Paste & Read.
⚙︎ Auto-receive (optional) — read my newest block without pasting
Polls every 15s; speaks only when the text changes. Needs one tap on “Start” to unlock audio. Leave off to use paste/pull mode. The Pull button also uses this URL.
ℹ︎ Tips
• Tap any sentence in the reading view to jump playback there.
• Word-by-word highlight works on Android/desktop; on iPhone you’ll get sentence highlight (Safari limitation).
• iPhone: download a better voice once — Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → English → “Enhanced/Premium”.
• Keep the screen on while listening (web TTS stops on lock).
• If silent on iPhone, flip the side mute switch off.
